An AI writer is software that makes use of artificial intelligence to predict text based on input that you supply it. AI writers are capable of creating marketing copy, landing pages, blog topic ideas, slogans, brand names, lyrics, and even full blog posts. When people hear of this for the first time, they are understandably skeptical until they try an AI writer for themselves. For the most part, AI writers make the working lives of those that use them much easier. It does a lot of the creative heavy-lifting, so to speak, which allows the user to save time by focusing on other important matters in their professional lives.
